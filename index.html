All about Animals and Oragami

The Camel

  • Camels have fluffy ears
  • Camels run great on sand because of their foot design
  • They drink as much water as possible

The Chameleon

  • Chameleons are a type of reptile that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Chameleons communicate through skin change
  • Some chameleons have long tails that they can wrap around tree branches

The Pigeon

  • Pigeons are very intelligent
  • They get around by flying with their flexible wings
  • Their hearing is excellent

The Bear

  • Bears are very fluffy
  • Bears are usually huge which makes them look dangerous to other animals
  • Bears hybernate throughout the winter

The Panda

  • Pands are very fluffy
  • They spend a lot of time eating
  • Bamboo is critical to their diet

The Cicada

  • Cicadas are very loud
  • They live long and shed lots of skin
  • Cicads lay many eggs
